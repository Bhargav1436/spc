Key Personnel

Mr. Madhusudhana

Managing Director
30+ Yrs Experience

Mr. Madhusudhana has one such inspiring story. Hailing from Kolar, he has completed his Bachelor's of Engineering (Electrical & Electronics) from the Renowned Bangalore University. Having 30+ Years of experience, Mr. Madhusudhana has donned numerous hats from being a Project Manager at SAPA Extrusion India Pvt Ltd and handling Green Field Projects to being a Project Manager at ASIN Automotive Karnataka in the Automobile & Aeronautics sector. After taking up various roles at different levels, Mr. Madhusudhana has cultivated his experience & expertise to grow SPC and take it to impeccable heights of success.

Mr. Sharanbasava Raj Patil

General Manager
20+ Yrs Experience

With a rich Experience of 20+ Years in the Field of Construction Engineering and Project Management, Mr. Sharanbasava Raj Patil is a qualified Project Management Certified Professional. He is a Chartered Engineer and Member of The Institution of Engineers & Valuers, India. He has completed his bachelor's in Engineering (Civil) from the prestigious Vishveshwaraiah Technological University, Bangalore. Having worked as Construction Manager with Tata Projects Ltd & Senior Manager at CBRE South Asia Pvt Ltd, Mr. Patil is lending his rich experience and expertise with SPC as General Manager - Projects.

Mr. Dr. Sudharshan B S

Technical Consultant
30+ Yrs Experience

A Structural Engineering Doctorate from Bangalore India is been in the field of Structural / Civil Engineering for over 30 years. Professional career started with Tata Consulting Engineers, Bangalore as Design Engineer and is currently the Principal Consultant for SPC. He has designed Major structures like ware house, industrial structures, Bridges, highways and Many other Civil Engineering Structures. He has worked on projects located across INDIA, USA, UK, UAE, Korea, Japan, Indonesia, Thailand, Sudan, Maldives, Senegal and Mozambique. A recipient of many merit awards from various organizations / institutions like TCE, BHEL, Turnipseed International (USA), CASE Engineered Lumber (USA), GIBCO, etc., He is a life member of ICI (Indian Concrete Institute) and ACCE (Association of Consulting Civil Engineers).

Mr. Anant

Structural Consultant
M.Tech in Structural Engineering

Mr. Anant has been very significant in the success ladder for SPC as their Structural Consultant. Mr. Anant has modest and updated approach in Structural Engineering and is a qualified M.Tech Professional in Structural Engineering. He has worked on some of the prestigious projects of SPC.
